Parents & Teachers Guide

Resources and guidance to help parents and teachers nurture children in the fear of the Lord.

Daily Devotion Tips

  • Read a Bible verse together daily
  • Pray with children in simple words
  • Encourage questions about faith
  • Share personal testimonies appropriately

Teaching Methods

  • Use stories and vivid illustrations
  • Encourage gentle memorization
  • Use songs and interactive play
  • Keep lessons short and engaging

Weekly Schedule

  • Create a consistent routine
  • Review the Weekly Lesson together
  • Practice the Memory Verse daily
  • End the week with a family quiz

Spiritual Growth

  • Model Christ-like behavior
  • Teach kindness, patience, and love
  • Encourage obedience to God's word
  • Celebrate spiritual milestones
