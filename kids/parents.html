Parents & Teachers Guide

Resources and guidance to help parents and teachers nurture children in the fear of the Lord.

  • Pray with children in simple words
  • Encourage questions about faith

Teaching Methods

  • Use stories and illustrations
  • Encourage memorization gently
  • Use songs and repetition

Weekly Lesson Plan

  • Bible story of the week
  • Memory verse
  • Short activity or quiz

Spiritual Growth

  • Model Christian behavior
  • Teach kindness and obedience
  • Encourage daily prayer
